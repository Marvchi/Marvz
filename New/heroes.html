Heroes

There are over 120 heroes in Dota 2, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. Heroes are divided into two teams, Radiant and Dire.

Radiant heroes are typically associated with light and order, while Dire heroes are typically associated with darkness and chaos.

Heroes can be played in a variety of roles, including carry, support, initiator, and ganker.

Carry heroes are typically played in the safe lane and are responsible for dealing damage and carrying the teamin the late game.

Support heroes are typically played in the support lane and are responsible for providing support to their carry heroes.

Initiator heroes are typically played in the offlane and are responsible for initiating teamfights.

Gankers are typically played in the jungle and are responsible for ganking enemy heroes.
