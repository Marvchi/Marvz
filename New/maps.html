Maps

Dota 2 is played on a variety of maps, each with their own unique features. The most popular map is the standard map, which is a three-lane map with a river running through the middle.

Other maps include the Diretide map, which is a Halloween-themed map with pumpkins and candy, and the Overthrow map, which is a fast-paced map designed for shorter games.
