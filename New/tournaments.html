Tournaments

Dota 2 is one of the most popular esports games in the world, and there are a number of major tournaments held each year. The most prestigious tournament is The International, which is held annually and has a prize pool of over \$20 million.

Other major tournaments include the Dota 2 Majors, which are held four times a year, and the Dota 2 Champions League, which is a European-based tournament.
